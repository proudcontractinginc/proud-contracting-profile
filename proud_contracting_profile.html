Proud Contracting Inc.

Proud Contracting Inc. is a trade coordination and general contracting firm serving the Lower Mainland of British Columbia. We connect builders, developers, and property managers with vetted and reliable tradespeople for residential and commercial projects.

Our Services

We specialize in end-to-end trade coordination, project supervision, and code-compliant execution. Whether it's a custom home, multi-family development, or commercial build-out, we ensure every trade is delivered on schedule with quality assurance.

Service Areas

We operate across Metro Vancouver and the Fraser Valley, including Vancouver, Burnaby, Surrey, Langley, Maple Ridge, and surrounding cities.
